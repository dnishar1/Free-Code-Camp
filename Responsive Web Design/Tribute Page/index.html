Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi

The father of India
Indian activist and a follower of non-violence

    Here is the timeline of Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi's Life:

  • 1869 - Was born in Porbandar - Gujarat, India
  • 1881 - Marries Kasturbai Makhanji in an Arranged Child Marriage at the age of 13
  • 1888 - Travels to London to train as a barrister
  • 1893 - Ejected from a South African train motivating him to fight for Indian rights in the british colony
  • 1906 - Introduces Non-violent protest, the philosophy of Satyagraha
  • 1915 - Receives hero's welcome upon returning to India from South Africa
  • 1920 - Gandhi Era of the Indian Independence Movement begins with the non-cooperation movement
  • 1930 - Embarks on the Salt Satyagraha
  • 1947 - United Kingdom passes the Indian Independence Act 1947
  • 1947 - Partition and Independence of India from the British rule
  • 1948 - Assassinated by Nathuram Godse
